About

Economics, evidence and quantitative strategy.

I am an Ecuadorian economist with a quantitative profile focused on applied econometrics, statistical modeling, data science, economic research and business analytics.

My work connects economic theory, empirical methods and data-driven decision-making. I am particularly interested in labour markets, poverty, inequality, education, development economics, public policy, financial risk, forecasting and business intelligence.

I use Python, R, Stata, SQL, Power BI and Excel to clean data, build models, evaluate results and communicate findings clearly.
